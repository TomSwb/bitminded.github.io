## Value Proposition

- **Artisanal apps + human guidance under one roof:** Pair handcrafted software with judgment-free tech coaching so people gain tools *and* the confidence to use them.
- **Personal touch at human scale:** Small, purposeful apps and calm, one-to-one support delivered directly by the maker.
- **Commission-to-catalog model:** Clients who commission apps get them free (or discounted); I retain rights and add them to a growing catalog for others to enjoy.
- **Ethical, transparent craft:** Like Swiss artisans, I build sustainably and teach safe digital habits so trust compounds over time.

---

## Customer Segments

- Individuals with niche needs (personal productivity, lifestyle tools)
- Tech-anxious learners who want patient coaching to feel safe online (students, seniors, career shifters)
- Families seeking shared digital literacy rituals and healthy tech boundaries
- Small businesses & creatives needing lightweight, affordable, custom software
- Local communities & associations (schools, clubs, NGOs) who value tailored solutions and in-person guidance
- Supporters of indie/ethical software who choose artisan-made over corporate

---

## Channels

- Personal website (homepage as the atelier for apps and guidance stories)
- Direct outreach (LinkedIn, forums, local entrepreneur circles, maker communities)
- Digital marketplaces (Gumroad, App Store, Google Play, GitHub Sponsors)
- 1:1 and small-group sessions (video, phone, in-person home visits, community centers)
- Workshops, meetups, word of mouth (like artisans at local markets)

## Customer Relationships

- Transparent and human: direct contact with the maker/coach
- Judgment-free, bedside guidance that builds trust before touching a button
- Early adopters feel like patrons supporting the craft and evangelists for calm tech habits
- Ongoing updates, personal session recaps, and rituals/playbooks tailored to each client
