Cookie Notice

Last updated: November 2025

1. Summary

BitMinded uses a minimal set of cookies strictly necessary to operate your account and remember your preferences. We do not deploy advertising, tracking, or social media cookies.

2. Essential Cookies

Name Purpose Provider Expiration
sb-access-token Maintains your authenticated session securely across bitminded.ch subdomains. Supabase (BitMinded) 7 days
sb-refresh-token Refreshes your session so you remain signed in without re-entering credentials. Supabase (BitMinded) 30 days

These cookies are essential for account security. Disabling them prevents login from functioning.

3. Preference Storage

We use localStorage to remember your language selection, theme preference, and mission toggle state. This data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties.

4. Analytics and Third-Party Tools

No analytics or marketing trackers are currently active. If we add privacy-respecting analytics in the future, this notice will be updated and, if needed, cookie consent options will be presented.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies via your browser settings. Essential cookies may be deleted by clearing your browser cache, but you will need to sign in again.

6. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@bitminded.ch.
