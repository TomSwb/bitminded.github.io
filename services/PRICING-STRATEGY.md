## üìä Volume Strategy Analysis

### Strategy: Lower Price = Higher Volume

**Philosophy:** Focus on quantity of subscribers vs. amount per subscriber. Make catalog access accessible to "petite gens" (ordinary people), not just businesses.

### Revenue Targets

| Price Point | Subscribers Needed | Monthly Revenue | Annual Revenue (after tax ~25%) |
|-------------|-------------------|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| CHF 5/month | 1,000 | CHF 5,000 | CHF 45,000 |
| **CHF 8/month** | **625** | **CHF 5,000** | **CHF 45,000** |
| CHF 10/month | 500 | CHF 5,000 | CHF 45,000 |
| CHF 18/month (current) | 278 | CHF 5,000 | CHF 45,000 |

### Recommended: CHF 8/month

**Rationale:**
- ‚úÖ Accessible to "petite gens" (price of a coffee)
- ‚úÖ Achievable volume (625 subscribers is realistic)
- ‚úÖ Better economics than CHF 5/month
- ‚úÖ Lower barrier than CHF 18/month
- ‚úÖ Sustainable for Vaud living costs

### Calculations (CHF 8/month)

**Gross Revenue:**
- 625 subscribers √ó CHF 8 = CHF 5,000/month

**After Stripe Fees (3%):**
- CHF 5,000 - CHF 150 = CHF 4,850/month

**After Tax (~25%):**
- CHF 4,850 √ó 0.75 = CHF 3,638/month = CHF 43,650/year

**Verdict:** Sustainable for one person in Vaud, especially with commissions and tech support services.

## üéØ Project-Based Pricing Structure

### Commissioning: Project Complexity Scale

**Key Insight:** Apps can vary significantly in complexity. Simple apps can be built in 1-2 weeks, while complex apps may take 6+ weeks.

#### Simple App
- **Duration:** 1-2 weeks
- **Price:** CHF 450-750
- **Perfect for:** Personal tools, simple automations, focused utilities
- **Delivery:** 1-2 weeks

#### Standard App
- **Duration:** 2-4 weeks
- **Price:** CHF 850-1,250
- **Perfect for:** Small business tools, multi-feature apps
- **Delivery:** 2-4 weeks

#### Complex App
- **Duration:** 6+ weeks
- **Price:** CHF 1,350-1,950
- **Perfect for:** Multi-user systems, advanced integrations
- **Delivery:** 6+ weeks

### Current Pricing Breakdown

**Effective Hourly Rate:**
- Simple App (CHF 450-750 for 1-2 weeks): ~CHF 40-50/hour
- Standard App (CHF 850-1,250 for 2-4 weeks): ~CHF 40-50/hour
- Complex App (CHF 1,350-1,950 for 6+ weeks): ~CHF 40-50/hour
- **Assessment:** Appropriate for "petite gens" market (accessible, not premium agency pricing)

**Project-Based Pricing:**
- Simple App: CHF 450-750 (1-2 weeks) - Personal tools, simple automations, focused utilities
- Standard App: CHF 850-1,250 (2-4 weeks) - Small business tools, multi-feature apps
- Complex App: CHF 1,350-1,950 (6+ weeks) - Multi-user systems, advanced integrations
- Commission a Feature: CHF 20-150 (depending on difficulty) - New features for existing catalog tools

## üîë Key Decisions & Rationale

### 1. Pricing for "Petite Gens" (Not Premium Agencies)

**Decision:** Keep commissioning at CHF 40-50/hour effective rate (CHF 450-1,950 per project)

**Rationale:**
- Focus on accessibility, not premium positioning
- Serve individuals and small projects
- Build volume through accessibility
- Differentiate from expensive agencies (CHF 80-150/hour)
- Simplified pricing structure (project-based, not sprint-based)

### 2. Volume Strategy for Catalog

**Decision:** Lower catalog pricing to CHF 8/month (from CHF 18/month)

**Rationale:**
- Focus on quantity over price-per-subscriber
- Make it accessible to "petite gens"
- Lower barrier to entry = more subscribers
- 625 subscribers at CHF 8/month = CHF 5,000/month (same revenue as 278 at CHF 18/month)

### 3. Project-Based Pricing for Commissioning

**Decision:** Simplified to project-based pricing (Simple/Standard/Complex apps)

**Rationale:**
- Apps vary significantly in complexity
- Simple apps can be built in 1-2 weeks
- Standard apps take 2-4 weeks
- Complex apps take 6+ weeks
- Project-based pricing is clearer and more accessible than sprint-based terminology
- Discovery is now included in Personal Project Intake (CHF 20, credited)
- Customization replaced by "Commission a Feature" for existing catalog tools

### 4. Tech Support Services Complement Commissioning

**Decision:** Keep tech support services at current pricing (CHF 45-180)

**Rationale:**
- Builds relationships and trust
- Generates commissioning leads
- Accessible pricing for families and individuals
- Fits Vaud market expectations

### 5. Deferred Services (Not Implemented)

**Decision:** Defer retainers, workshops, and consulting services

**Rationale:**
- Focus on core services first
- Build volume with catalog and tech support
- Add advanced services later if needed
- Keep scope manageable for one person

## üîÆ Future Considerations

### Scaling Considerations

1. **Customer Acquisition:**
   - Need steady marketing/inbound for volume strategy
   - Low price helps word-of-mouth
   - Consider freemium or trial options

2. **Churn Management:**
   - Lower price may reduce commitment
   - Focus on value and engagement
   - Regular updates and new tools

3. **Support Scalability:**
   - More subscribers = more support questions
   - Automate where possible
   - Use community/forums

4. **Content Velocity:**
   - Regular new tools drive retention
   - Catalog must grow consistently
   - Consider user-generated content

### Potential Adjustments

1. **If Volume Strategy Succeeds:**
   - Consider adding more tiers (Individual, Family, Business)
   - Add more features to Supporter Tier
   - Consider annual-only pricing for deeper discount

2. **If Volume Strategy Struggles:**
   - Consider raising price to CHF 10/month
   - Focus on quality over quantity
   - Emphasize value proposition

3. **If Commissioning Demand Grows:**
   - Consider raising prices slightly (CHF 50-60/hour)
   - Focus on quality clients
   - Add premium tier for faster delivery

### Deferred Services (Future Implementation)

- **Build + Guide Retainer:** From CHF 450/month
- **Patron Bundle:** CHF 750/quarter
- **Family Plan Retainer:** CHF 160-320/month
- **Workshops / Consulting:** CHF 100-300/session or CHF 2k-20k/project

**Rationale:** Focus on core services first, add advanced services later if needed.
