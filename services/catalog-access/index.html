Tool Access

Access to our collection of artisanal apps. Single tool licenses grant lifetime access to one tool, while memberships unlock everything.

Updates

All tools receive regular updates. All licenses include updates for the purchased tool, while memberships get also new tools automatically.

Release Notes

Stay informed about updates. Members receive private release notes with detailed information about new features and improvements.

Free Trial

Try all tools risk-free before committing. Subscription tiers include a free trial period to explore the catalog.

Feature Voting

Supporter tier members can vote on new features and improvements, and also propose their own features to any tool, helping shape the future of our catalog.

Accessibility Initiative

Your support directly contributes to making our tools accessible to as many people as possible. This includes funding accessibility improvements, supporting users with financial constraints, and ensuring our artisanal development reaches everyone who needs it.
