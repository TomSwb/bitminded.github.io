Licensing Terms

Perpetual License

You receive a perpetual-use license for your commissioned tool. Deploy it internally forever with no recurring fees or subscription required.

IP Retention & Catalog Rights

We retain intellectual property rights and the ability to adapt your tool for the public catalog. You keep full use rights for your commissioned version.

Updates & Maintenance

Your commissioned tool receives updates and maintenance during the build process. Post-delivery updates for your specific version can be discussed separately.
