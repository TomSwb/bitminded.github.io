Tech Support

Tech Confidence Coaching focuses on building digital rituals and confidence over time through structured sessions. Quick Tech Help is perfect for immediate troubleshooting or setup needs (45-minute sessions). Family Tech Connection offers group sessions covering shared devices, parental controls, and healthy digital habits, available remotely or in-person.

We start by understanding your needs and goals. Then we work together step-by-step, explaining everything in plain language with no judgment. After each session, you'll receive annotated notes summarizing what we covered, checklists for future reference, and personalized recommendations for building healthy tech habits.

Most sessions work great remotely via video call. For hands-on device setup or family sessions, we offer in-home visits or meet-ups at your location. Home Visit Essentials (CHF 120 + travel, 90 minutes) and Onsite Visits (CHF 180 + travel) are available for in-person support.

We can create custom guidance packages based on your needs. Contact us to discuss ongoing support options.
