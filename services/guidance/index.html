What to Expect

Session Flow

We start by understanding your needs and goals. Then we work together step-by-step, explaining everything in plain language. No judgment, just patient guidance.

What You'll Receive

Annotated notes summarizing what we covered, checklists for future reference, and personalized recommendations for building healthy tech habits.

Remote vs. In-Person

Most sessions work great remotely via video call. For hands-on device setup or family sessions, we offer in-home visits or meet-ups at your location.
